About Us

What is Chocolate Dreams?


It all started with one choclate lover who wanted to turn her fantasy into a reality. Studying the art of chocolate and experimenting with flavors were just the start. Different designs, wrappings, and shapes all started to take place and by 2017 Chocolate Dreams was up and running.


Chocolate Dreams is a family company, with three generations worth of ideas and creativity. Taste tests take place at all family gatherings and holiday dinners. Family and love is at the heart and center of this company.


Located in Salisbury, Maryland, you can find all your chocolate desires here at Chocolate Dreams. Don't see what you are looking for, feel free to schedule an appointment with us. We do our best here at Chocolate Dreams to give customers endless types of products. Want something customized? Don't hesitate to ask!


We hope to see you real soon here at Chocolate Dreams.
